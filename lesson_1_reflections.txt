What do you think are the pros and cons of manually choosing when to create a commit, like you do in Git, vs having versions automatically saved, like Google Docs does?
Manual save:
	Pros: More logical iteration between versions, and significantly easier to locate bug. 
	Cons: May lose some data if computer crashes.
Auto save:
	Pros: Significantly reduce risk of losing data.
	Cons: Somewhat more difficult to locate bug between versions.
